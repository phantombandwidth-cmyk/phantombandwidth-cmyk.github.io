A fast PDF reader and annotation tool. Highlights, notes, signatures, and full-text search — open documents from anywhere, annotate them privately.

What's inside
Everything you need in a PDF app

7 Annotation Tools

Highlight, draw, add notes, place shapes, whiteout, sign, and checkmark. Long-press any annotation to delete, recolor, or move it.

Full-Text Search

Search inside any document. Folio builds a text index the first time you search — results appear instantly after that, with no background syncing.

Annotation Export

Export a flattened PDF with annotations burned in, or export your notes as Markdown, JSON, or CSV for Obsidian, Notion, or a spreadsheet.

Multi-Document Tabs

Keep several PDFs open at once. Each tab preserves its own page, zoom, and scroll position across sessions.

Private by Design

Folio never stores, syncs, or transmits your documents or annotations. Open files from your device or any cloud service — what you annotate stays on your phone.

3 Themes

Light, Dark, and Slate. All selectable in Settings with live preview. Font selection included. Preferences persist across sessions.
